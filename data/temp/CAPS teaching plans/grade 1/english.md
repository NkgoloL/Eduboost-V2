# Grade 1 English Home Language (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: Phonics (a, e, i, o, u)
- Week 2: Reading short words
- Week 3: Listening & speaking (story retelling)
- Week 4: Writing simple sentences
- Week 5: Vocabulary building
- Week 6: Grammar (nouns)
- Week 7: Creative writing (short story)
- Week 8: Revision & assessment

## Term 2
- Week 1: Phonics (consonant blends)
- Week 2: Reading comprehension (short passages)
- Week 3: Listening & speaking (dialogues)
- Week 4: Writing (paragraphs)
- Week 5: Grammar (verbs)
- Week 6: Vocabulary expansion
- Week 7: Creative writing (poems)
- Week 8: Revision & assessment

## Term 3
- Week 1: Phonics (long vowels)
- Week 2: Reading comprehension (stories)
- Week 3: Listening & speaking (debates)
- Week 4: Writing (letters)
- Week 5: Grammar (adjectives)
- Week 6: Vocabulary (synonyms/antonyms)
- Week 7: Creative writing (descriptive)
- Week 8: Revision & assessment

## Term 4
- Week 1: Phonics (complex sounds)
- Week 2: Reading comprehension (non-fiction)
- Week 3: Listening & speaking (presentations)
- Week 4: Writing (reports)
- Week 5: Grammar (punctuation)
- Week 6: Vocabulary (idioms)
- Week 7: Creative writing (narrative)
- Week 8: Revision & final assessment
