# Grade 1 Mathematics (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: Counting 1–10, number recognition
- Week 2: Addition within 10
- Week 3: Subtraction within 10
- Week 4: Shapes and patterns
- Week 5: Measurement (length, mass)
- Week 6: Time (days of week)
- Week 7: Money (coins)
- Week 8: Revision & assessment

## Term 2
- Week 1: Place value (tens and ones)
- Week 2: Addition within 20
- Week 3: Subtraction within 20
- Week 4: Fractions (halves, quarters)
- Week 5: Geometry (2D shapes)
- Week 6: Measurement (capacity)
- Week 7: Data handling (pictographs)
- Week 8: Revision & assessment

## Term 3
- Week 1: Counting to 50
- Week 2: Multiplication (groups of 2, 5)
- Week 3: Division (sharing equally)
- Week 4: Fractions (thirds)
- Week 5: Geometry (3D shapes)
- Week 6: Measurement (time: hours)
- Week 7: Data handling (bar graphs)
- Week 8: Revision & assessment

## Term 4
- Week 1: Counting to 100
- Week 2: Addition/subtraction with regrouping
- Week 3: Multiplication (groups of 10)
- Week 4: Division (remainders)
- Week 5: Geometry (symmetry)
- Week 6: Measurement (temperature)
- Week 7: Probability (everyday events)
- Week 8: Revision & final assessment
