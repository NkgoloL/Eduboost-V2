# Grade 1 Sepedi (FAL)

- Week 1: Greetings & introductions
- Week 2: Vocabulary (family)
- Week 3: Listening & speaking (songs)
- Week 4: Reading simple words
- Week 5: Writing (labels)
- Week 6: Grammar (nouns)
- Week 7: Oral storytelling
- Week 8: Revision & assessment

- Week 5: Grammar (adjectives)

- Week 1: Vocabulary (transport)

- Week 3: Reading comprehension (non-fiction)
