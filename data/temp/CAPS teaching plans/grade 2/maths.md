# Grade 2 Mathematics (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: Counting to 200
- Week 2: Place value (hundreds, tens, ones)
- Week 3: Addition/subtraction within 100
- Week 4: Multiplication (2, 5, 10 tables)
- Week 5: Division (sharing, grouping)
- Week 6: Fractions (halves, quarters, thirds)
- Week 7: Geometry (2D shapes, symmetry)
- Week 8: Revision & assessment

## Term 2
- Week 1: Counting backwards
- Week 2: Expanded notation
- Week 3: Addition/subtraction with regrouping
- Week 4: Multiplication (3, 4 tables)
- Week 5: Division with remainders
- Week 6: Measurement (length, mass, capacity)
- Week 7: Data handling (bar graphs)
- Week 8: Revision & assessment

## Term 3
- Week 1: Counting to 500
- Week 2: Place value (thousands)
- Week 3: Fractions (fifths, tenths)
- Week 4: Geometry (3D shapes)
- Week 5: Measurement (time: hours, minutes)
- Week 6: Money (rand & cents)
- Week 7: Probability (likely/unlikely events)
- Week 8: Revision & assessment

## Term 4
- Week 1: Counting to 1000
- Week 2: Addition/subtraction with thousands
- Week 3: Multiplication (6, 7 tables)
- Week 4: Division (long division basics)
- Week 5: Geometry (angles, triangles)
- Week 6: Measurement (temperature)
- Week 7: Data handling (pie charts)
- Week 8: Revision & final assessment
