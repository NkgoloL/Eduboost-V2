# Grade 2 Sepedi First Additional Language (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: Dumedišo le boitsebišo
- Week 2: Polelo ya malapa
- Week 3: Go bala mantšu a bonolo
- Week 4: Go ngwala dipolelo tše khutšo
- Week 5: Go theeletša le go bolela (dikanegelo)
- Week 6: Sebopego sa mantšu (maina)
- Week 7: Dithoko le dipina
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko & tekolo

## Term 2
- Week 1: Polelo ya dilo tša sekolo
- Week 2: Go bala ditemana tše khutšo
- Week 3: Go ngwala (diparagarafa)
- Week 4: Go theeletša le go bolela (dipoledi)
- Week 5: Sebopego sa mantšu (mašito)
- Week 6: Polelo ya diphoofolo
- Week 7: Dithoko tša molomo
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko & tekolo

## Term 3
- Week 1: Polelo ya dijo
- Week 2: Go bala dikanegelo
- Week 3: Go ngwala (makwalo)
- Week 4: Go theeletša le go bolela (ditaelo)
- Week 5: Sebopego sa mantšu (mahlatse)
- Week 6: Polelo ya leratadima
- Week 7: Dipontšho tša molomo
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko & tekolo

## Term 4
- Week 1: Polelo ya dipalangwa
- Week 2: Go bala ditemana tša tsebo
- Week 3: Go ngwala (dintlha)
- Week 4: Go theeletša le go bolela (dikgokagano)
- Week 5: Sebopego sa mantšu (dipontšo)
- Week 6: Polelo ya setšo
- Week 7: Dikanegelo tša molomo
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko & tekolo ya mafelelo
