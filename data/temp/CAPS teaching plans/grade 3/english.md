# Grade 3 English Home Language (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: Phonics (vowel digraphs), Listening & Speaking (news)
- Week 2: Reading (fiction), Writing (personal recount)
- Week 3: Phonics (consonant blends), Handwriting
- Week 4: Language Structures (nouns & pronouns)
- Week 5: Listening & Speaking (giving instructions)
- Week 6: Reading (poetry), Writing (simple poem)
- Week 7: Phonics (silent 'e'), Vocabulary building
- Week 8: Language Structures (verbs: present tense)
- Week 9: Revision
- Week 10: Assessment

## Term 2
- Week 1: Phonics (long vowel sounds), Reading (non-fiction)
- Week 2: Writing (informational paragraph)
- Week 3: Language Structures (adjectives)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (storytelling)
- Week 5: Reading (fables), Writing (dialogue)
- Week 6: Phonics (suffixes -ed, -ing)
- Week 7: Language Structures (conjunctions)
- Week 8: Vocabulary (synonyms and antonyms)
- Week 9: Revision
- Week 10: Assessment

## Term 3
- Week 1: Phonics (prefixes), Reading (drama/plays)
- Week 2: Writing (short script)
- Week 3: Language Structures (past tense)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (reporting)
- Week 5: Reading (informational texts), Writing (lists)
- Week 6: Phonics (complex sounds), Spelling
- Week 7: Language Structures (prepositions)
- Week 8: Vocabulary (dictionary skills)
- Week 9: Revision
- Week 10: Assessment

## Term 4
- Week 1: Phonics (revision), Reading (longer stories)
- Week 2: Writing (narrative essay)
- Week 3: Language Structures (punctuation)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (presentation)
- Week 5: Reading (comics), Writing (captions)
- Week 6: Vocabulary (idiomatic expressions)
- Week 7: Language Structures (future tense)
- Week 8: Revision
- Week 9: Final Revision
- Week 10: Final Assessment
