# Grade 3 Life Skills (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: About me (Personal strengths)
- Week 2: Feelings and emotions
- Week 3: Health and hygiene
- Week 4: Safety at home and school
- Week 5: Physical Education (Basic movements)
- Week 6: Creative Arts (Drawing and painting)
- Week 7: Environment (Planting)
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 2
- Week 1: Rights and responsibilities
- Week 2: Conflict resolution
- Week 3: Nutrition (Healthy eating)
- Week 4: Physical Education (Games and rules)
- Week 5: Creative Arts (Music and dance)
- Week 6: Religion and culture (Festivals)
- Week 7: Recycling and waste
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 3
- Week 1: Public safety (Road safety)
- Week 2: First Aid basics
- Week 3: Careers and work
- Week 4: Physical Education (Athletics)
- Week 5: Creative Arts (Drama and performance)
- Week 6: Space (The solar system)
- Week 7: Water safety
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 4
- Week 1: Pollution (Causes and effects)
- Week 2: Conserving electricity
- Week 3: Emotional well-being (Resilience)
- Week 4: Physical Education (Team sports)
- Week 5: Creative Arts (Mixed media)
- Week 6: Social justice (Inclusivity)
- Week 7: Final Revision
- Week 8: Final Assessment
