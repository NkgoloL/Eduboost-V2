# Grade 3 Mathematics (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: Numbers to 500, place value
- Week 2: Addition and subtraction within 500
- Week 3: Multiplication (2, 3, 4, 5, 10 tables)
- Week 4: Division (grouping and sharing)
- Week 5: Money (shopping problems)
- Week 6: Geometric patterns
- Week 7: 2D shapes (properties)
- Week 8: Measurement (length)
- Week 9: Data handling (tally charts)
- Week 10: Revision & Assessment

## Term 2
- Week 1: Numbers to 750, ordering
- Week 2: Addition and subtraction with carrying
- Week 3: Multiplication (repeated addition)
- Week 4: Division (repeated subtraction)
- Week 5: Fractions (halves, quarters, eighths)
- Week 6: Time (analogue and digital)
- Week 7: 3D objects (naming and sorting)
- Week 8: Measurement (mass)
- Week 9: Data handling (bar graphs)
- Week 10: Revision & Assessment

## Term 3
- Week 1: Numbers to 1000, place value
- Week 2: Mental mathematics (doubling and halving)
- Week 3: Multiplication (multi-step problems)
- Week 4: Division (long division intro)
- Week 5: Fractions (sharing equally)
- Week 6: Time (calendars)
- Week 7: Symmetry (line of symmetry)
- Week 8: Measurement (capacity)
- Week 9: Data handling (interpreting data)
- Week 10: Revision & Assessment

## Term 4
- Week 1: Numbers to 1000, counting patterns
- Week 2: Addition and subtraction (large numbers)
- Week 3: Multiplication and division (inverse operations)
- Week 4: Fractions (problem solving)
- Week 5: Money (profit and loss)
- Week 6: Geometry (position and movement)
- Week 7: Measurement (temperature)
- Week 8: Data handling (project work)
- Week 9: Final Revision
- Week 10: Final Assessment
