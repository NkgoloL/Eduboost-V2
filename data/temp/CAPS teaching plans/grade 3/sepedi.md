# Grade 3 Sepedi First Additional Language (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: Madume le boitsebišo (Greetings and introductions)
- Week 2: Polelo ya ka gae (Home language vocabulary)
- Week 3: Go bala mantšu le dipolelo (Reading words and sentences)
- Week 4: Go ngwala (Writing simple sentences)
- Week 5: Sebopego sa polelo (Grammar: Maina)
- Week 6: Go theeletša le go bolela (Listening and speaking)
- Week 7: Mareo a mmele (Body parts)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko (Assessment)

## Term 2
- Week 1: Polelo ya sekolo (School vocabulary)
- Week 2: Go bala dikanegelo (Reading stories)
- Week 3: Go ngwala (Writing paragraphs)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Grammar: Mašito)
- Week 5: Diphoofolo (Animals)
- Week 6: Mehlare le maloba (Trees and flowers)
- Week 7: Go theeletša le go bolela (Songs)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko (Assessment)

## Term 3
- Week 1: Dijo (Food)
- Week 2: Go bala (Reading informational texts)
- Week 3: Go ngwala (Writing letters)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Grammar: Madiri)
- Week 5: Boemo bja leratadima (Weather)
- Week 6: Dipalangwa (Transport)
- Week 7: Dipapadi (Sports)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko (Assessment)

## Term 4
- Week 1: Setšo le ditšo (Culture and traditions)
- Week 2: Go bala (Poetry)
- Week 3: Go ngwala (Creative writing)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Revision)
- Week 5: Melao ya tsela (Road rules)
- Week 6: Go theeletša le go bolela (Presentations)
- Week 7: Boitokišetšo bja ditlhahlobo (Exam prep)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko ya mafelelo (Final assessment)
