# Grade 4 English Home Language (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: Listening & Speaking (Group discussion)
- Week 2: Reading (Short story), Writing (Personal letter)
- Week 3: Language Structures (Sentence structure)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (Oral presentation)
- Week 5: Reading (Poetry), Writing (Poem)
- Week 6: Language Structures (Verbs: Present tense)
- Week 7: Reading (Folklore), Writing (Story rewrite)
- Week 8: Language Structures (Pronouns)
- Week 9: Revision
- Week 10: Assessment

## Term 2
- Week 1: Listening & Speaking (Interview skills)
- Week 2: Reading (News article), Writing (Simple report)
- Week 3: Language Structures (Adjectives and Adverbs)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (Debating)
- Week 5: Reading (Play script), Writing (Dialogue)
- Week 6: Language Structures (Past tense)
- Week 7: Reading (Informational text), Writing (List/Summary)
- Week 8: Language Structures (Conjunctions)
- Week 9: Revision
- Week 10: Assessment

## Term 3
- Week 1: Listening & Speaking (Telling a joke/story)
- Week 2: Reading (Novel excerpt), Writing (Narrative essay)
- Week 3: Language Structures (Prepositions)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (Book review)
- Week 5: Reading (Instructional text), Writing (Recipe/Guide)
- Week 6: Language Structures (Future tense)
- Week 7: Reading (Comic strips), Writing (Speech bubbles)
- Week 8: Language Structures (Punctuation: Quotation marks)
- Week 9: Revision
- Week 10: Assessment

## Term 4
- Week 1: Listening & Speaking (Formal speech)
- Week 2: Reading (Biography), Writing (Self-profile)
- Week 3: Language Structures (Subject-Verb agreement)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (Radio advertisement)
- Week 5: Reading (Advertisements), Writing (Poster)
- Week 6: Language Structures (Direct and Indirect speech)
- Week 7: Final Revision (All grammar)
- Week 8: Final Examination
- Week 9: Feedback and Moderation
- Week 10: End of Term activities
