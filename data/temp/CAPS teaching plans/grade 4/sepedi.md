# Grade 4 Sepedi First Additional Language (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: Go theeletša le go bolela (Greetings and social language)
- Week 2: Go bala le go kwišiša (Short stories)
- Week 3: Go ngwala le go hlama (Simple paragraphs)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Grammar: Nouns)
- Week 5: Mareo a sekolo (School terminology)
- Week 6: Diphoofolo (Animals and their sounds)
- Week 7: Go theeletša (Songs and rhymes)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko (Assessment)

## Term 2
- Week 1: Polelo ya gae (Home environment)
- Week 2: Go bala (Non-fiction texts)
- Week 3: Go ngwala (Instructions)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Grammar: Verbs)
- Week 5: Mehlare le dijalo (Trees and plants)
- Week 6: Boemo bja leratadima (Weather)
- Week 7: Go bolela (Role play)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko (Assessment)

## Term 3
- Week 1: Dijo le go phela gabotse (Food and health)
- Week 2: Go bala (Folk tales)
- Week 3: Go ngwala (Creative stories)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Grammar: Adjectives)
- Week 5: Dipalangwa (Means of transport)
- Week 6: Dipapadi (Sports and games)
- Week 7: Go theeletša (Poetry)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko (Assessment)

## Term 4
- Week 1: Setšo sa rena (Our culture)
- Week 2: Go bala (News articles)
- Week 3: Go ngwala (Letters)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Revision)
- Week 5: Boitshwaro (Behaviour and ethics)
- Week 6: Dithoko (Traditional praises)
- Week 7: Boitokišetšo bja ditlhahlobo (Exam prep)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko ya mafelelo (Final assessment)
