# Grade 5 English Home Language (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: Listening & Speaking (Group discussion: News)
- Week 2: Reading (Short story), Writing (Character description)
- Week 3: Language Structures (Verbs: Continuous tense)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (Storytelling with props)
- Week 5: Reading (Poetry), Writing (Acrostic poem)
- Week 6: Language Structures (Adjectives and Adverbs)
- Week 7: Reading (Mythology), Writing (Myth summary)
- Week 8: Language Structures (Punctuation: Commas and Colons)
- Week 9: Revision
- Week 10: Assessment

## Term 2
- Week 1: Listening & Speaking (Instructional oral)
- Week 2: Reading (Newspaper article), Writing (News report)
- Week 3: Language Structures (Prepositions and Phrases)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (Debate basics)
- Week 5: Reading (Drama excerpt), Writing (Dialogue script)
- Week 6: Language Structures (Past and Future continuous)
- Week 7: Reading (Informational text), Writing (Mind map/Summary)
- Week 8: Language Structures (Conjunctions: Compound sentences)
- Week 9: Revision
- Week 10: Assessment

## Term 3
- Week 1: Listening & Speaking (Prepared speech)
- Week 2: Reading (Historical fiction), Writing (Diary entry)
- Week 3: Language Structures (Direct and Indirect speech)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (Book talk)
- Week 5: Reading (Technical text), Writing (How-to guide)
- Week 6: Language Structures (Active and Passive voice - intro)
- Week 7: Reading (Comic strips), Writing (Original comic)
- Week 8: Language Structures (Punctuation: Hyphens and Dashes)
- Week 9: Revision
- Week 10: Assessment

## Term 4
- Week 1: Listening & Speaking (Persuasive oral)
- Week 2: Reading (Biographies), Writing (Autobiography)
- Week 3: Language Structures (Subject-Verb agreement)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (Role play: Conflict resolution)
- Week 5: Reading (Advertisements), Writing (Campaign poster)
- Week 6: Language Structures (Revision of all tenses)
- Week 7: Final Revision (Exam preparation)
- Week 8: Final Examination
- Week 9: Feedback and Moderation
- Week 10: Year-end activities
