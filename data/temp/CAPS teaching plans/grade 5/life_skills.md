# Grade 5 Life Skills (CAPS)

## Term 1: Personal and Social Well-being (PSW)
- Week 1: Positive self-concept (Strengths and weaknesses)
- Week 2: Dealing with success and failure
- Week 3: Peer pressure (How to say no)
- Week 4: Child abuse (Safety and reporting)
- Week 5: Physical Education (Basic fitness and games)
- Week 6: Creative Arts (Drama: Storytelling)
- Week 7: Creative Arts (Visual Arts: Painting)
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 2: Health and Environment
- Week 1: Nutrition (Balanced diet and vitamins)
- Week 2: Environmental health (Safe water and food)
- Week 3: Communicable diseases (Prevention)
- Week 4: Safety in and around water
- Week 5: Physical Education (Rhythmic movements)
- Week 6: Creative Arts (Music: Instrumental basics)
- Week 7: Creative Arts (Visual Arts: Drawing)
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 3: Rights and Responsibilities
- Week 1: National symbols of South Africa
- Week 2: Religious festivals and customs
- Week 3: Cultural diversity and tolerance
- Week 4: Rights and responsibilities (Bill of Rights)
- Week 5: Physical Education (Athletics and sportsmanship)
- Week 6: Creative Arts (Drama: Role play)
- Week 7: Creative Arts (Visual Arts: Sculpture)
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 4: Careers and Safety
- Week 1: Knowledge of the world of work (Careers)
- Week 2: Traffic safety (Passenger and pedestrian)
- Week 3: Substance abuse (Tobacco and alcohol)
- Week 4: Physical Education (Swimming/Field games)
- Week 5: Creative Arts (Music: Traditional songs)
- Week 6: Creative Arts (Visual Arts: Design)
- Week 7: Final Revision
- Week 8: Final Assessment
