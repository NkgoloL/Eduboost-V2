# Grade 5 Sepedi First Additional Language (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: Madume le polelo ya leago (Social language and greetings)
- Week 2: Go bala dikanegelo tše kopana (Short stories)
- Week 3: Go ngwala ditemana (Paragraph writing)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Grammar: Maina le Madiri)
- Week 5: Polelo ya mmele (Body parts and health)
- Week 6: Meloko le ditswala (Family and relations)
- Week 7: Go theeletša direto (Listening to praises)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko (Assessment)

## Term 2
- Week 1: Tikologo ya rena (Our environment)
- Week 2: Go bala dikanegelo tša go ruta (Fables)
- Week 3: Go ngwala ditaelo (Instructional writing)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Grammar: Mašito)
- Week 5: Dipalangwa le maeto (Transport and travel)
- Week 6: Boemo bja boso (Weather and climate)
- Week 7: Go bolela (Debates)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko (Assessment)

## Term 3
- Week 1: Dijo le matlotlo a tlhago (Food and natural resources)
- Week 2: Go bala ditaba (Reading news)
- Week 3: Go ngwala mangwalo (Letter writing)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Grammar: Mahlatse)
- Week 5: Dipapadi le boitšhidullo (Sports and exercise)
- Week 6: Dišupatsela (Directions)
- Week 7: Go theeletša (Traditional stories)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko (Assessment)

## Term 4
- Week 1: Setšo le bophelo (Culture and life)
- Week 2: Go bala dipoelo tša tsebo (Informational texts)
- Week 3: Go ngwala direto (Poetry writing)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Revision)
- Week 5: Boitshwaro bja batho (Human behavior)
- Week 6: Go bolela ka molomo (Oral presentations)
- Week 7: Boitokišetšo bja ditlhahlobo (Exam prep)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko ya mafelelo (Final assessment)
