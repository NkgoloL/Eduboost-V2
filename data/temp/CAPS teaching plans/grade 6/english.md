# Grade 6 English Home Language (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: Listening & Speaking (Group discussion: Social issues)
- Week 2: Reading (Short story), Writing (Character study)
- Week 3: Language Structures (Tenses: Perfect tense)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (Persuasive speech)
- Week 5: Reading (Poetry), Writing (Free verse poem)
- Week 6: Language Structures (Adjectives and Adverbs: Degrees of comparison)
- Week 7: Reading (Mythology/Legends), Writing (Original legend)
- Week 8: Language Structures (Punctuation: Semi-colons and Brackets)
- Week 9: Revision
- Week 10: Assessment

## Term 2
- Week 1: Listening & Speaking (Interviewing skills)
- Week 2: Reading (Newspaper article), Writing (Feature article)
- Week 3: Language Structures (Phrases and Clauses)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (Debate: Formal rules)
- Week 5: Reading (Drama), Writing (Play scene)
- Week 6: Language Structures (Modal verbs)
- Week 7: Reading (Informational text), Writing (Summary/Precis)
- Week 8: Language Structures (Conjunctions: Complex sentences)
- Week 9: Revision
- Week 10: Assessment

## Term 3
- Week 1: Listening & Speaking (Storytelling with dialogue)
- Week 2: Reading (Historical novel), Writing (Diary of a character)
- Week 3: Language Structures (Active and Passive voice)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (Panel discussion)
- Week 5: Reading (Technical instructions), Writing (User manual)
- Week 6: Language Structures (Direct and Indirect speech - revision)
- Week 7: Reading (Comics/Graphic novels), Writing (Storyboarding)
- Week 8: Language Structures (Punctuation: Elision and Apostrophe)
- Week 9: Revision
- Week 10: Assessment

## Term 4
- Week 1: Listening & Speaking (Keynote presentation)
- Week 2: Reading (Autobiographies), Writing (Personal narrative)
- Week 3: Language Structures (Revision of all grammar)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (Role play: Mediation)
- Week 5: Reading (Advertising), Writing (Marketing plan/Ad)
- Week 6: Language Structures (Figurative language)
- Week 7: Final Revision (Exam focus)
- Week 8: Final Examination
- Week 9: Feedback and Moderation
- Week 10: End of year celebration
