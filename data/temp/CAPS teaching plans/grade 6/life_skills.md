# Grade 6 Life Skills (CAPS)

## Term 1: Personal and Social Well-being (PSW)
- Week 1: Positive self-esteem (Influences)
- Week 2: Abilities and interests
- Week 3: Peer pressure and social groups
- Week 4: Child abuse (Safety and protection)
- Week 5: Physical Education (Basic fitness/Athletics)
- Week 6: Creative Arts (Drama: Mime and movement)
- Week 7: Creative Arts (Visual Arts: Drawing/Painting)
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 2: Health and Environment
- Week 1: Nutrition (Food groups and balanced meals)
- Week 2: Environmental health (Safe water/Waste management)
- Week 3: Communicable diseases (HIV/AIDS and TB)
- Week 4: Safety in and around water
- Week 5: Physical Education (Dance/Rhythmic movement)
- Week 6: Creative Arts (Music: Instrumental/Voice)
- Week 7: Creative Arts (Visual Arts: Sculpture/3D Art)
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 3: Rights and Responsibilities
- Week 1: National symbols and diversity
- Week 2: Religious festivals and beliefs
- Week 3: Gender equality and inclusivity
- Week 4: Children's rights and responsibilities
- Week 5: Physical Education (Invasion games/Teamwork)
- Week 6: Creative Arts (Drama: Role play/Puppetry)
- Week 7: Creative Arts (Visual Arts: Collage/Mosaic)
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 4: Careers and Safety
- Week 1: World of work (Careers and subjects)
- Week 2: Safety in the environment (Traffic/Disasters)
- Week 3: Substance abuse (Causes and effects)
- Week 4: Physical Education (Swimming/Summer games)
- Week 5: Creative Arts (Music: Traditional/Modern)
- Week 6: Creative Arts (Visual Arts: Design/Media)
- Week 7: Final Revision
- Week 8: Final Assessment
