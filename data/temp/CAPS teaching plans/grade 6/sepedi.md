# Grade 6 Sepedi First Additional Language (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: Madume le boitsebišo (Advanced greetings and social language)
- Week 2: Go bala dikanegelo tše telejana (Longer stories)
- Week 3: Go ngwala ditemana tša go hlalosa (Descriptive paragraphs)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Grammar: Maina, Madiri le Mašala)
- Week 5: Polelo ya mmele le go phela gabotse (Body and health)
- Week 6: Meloko le ditswala (Family and community relations)
- Week 7: Go theeletša direto le dikoša (Praises and songs)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko (Assessment)

## Term 2
- Week 1: Tikologo le tlhago (Environment and nature)
- Week 2: Go bala dikanegelo tša go ruta (Fables and morals)
- Week 3: Go ngwala ditaelo tše hlakaneletšego (Complex instructions)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Grammar: Mašito le Maatla)
- Week 5: Dipalangwa le maeto (Transport and travel)
- Week 6: Boemo bja boso le bophelo (Weather and life)
- Week 7: Go bolela (Debates and discussions)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko (Assessment)

## Term 3
- Week 1: Dijo le go kwišiša (Food and comprehension)
- Week 2: Go bala ditaba tša sekolong (School news)
- Week 3: Go ngwala mangwalo a semmušo (Formal letters)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Grammar: Mahlatse le Makgao)
- Week 5: Dipapadi le boitšhidullo (Sports and exercise)
- Week 6: Dišupatsela le diporo (Directions and maps)
- Week 7: Go theeletša dikanegelo tša setšo (Traditional stories)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko (Assessment)

## Term 4
- Week 1: Setšo le bophelo bja batho (Culture and human life)
- Week 2: Go bala dipoelo tša tsebo (Informational texts)
- Week 3: Go ngwala direto (Poetry writing)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Revision of grammar)
- Week 5: Boitshwaro le melao (Ethics and laws)
- Week 6: Go bolela ka molomo (Final oral presentations)
- Week 7: Boitokišetšo bja ditlhahlobo (Exam prep)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko ya mafelelo (Final assessment)
