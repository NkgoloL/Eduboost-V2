# Grade 7 Creative Arts (CAPS)

## Term 1: Visual Arts & Drama
- Week 1: Visual Arts (Observational drawing: Still life)
- Week 2: Visual Arts (Composition and layout)
- Week 3: Visual Arts (Colour theory: Tertiary colours)
- Week 4: Drama (Physical expression: Mime and gesture)
- Week 5: Drama (Voice: Modulation and projection)
- Week 6: Drama (Characterization and improvisation)
- Week 7: Integrating Art and Drama
- Week 8: Assessment

## Term 2: Music & Dance
- Week 1: Music (Musical notation: Stave and clefs)
- Week 2: Music (Rhythm: Complex time signatures)
- Week 3: Music (Listening to diverse South African genres)
- Week 4: Dance (Warm-up and flexibility training)
- Week 5: Dance (Rhythmic patterns and coordination)
- Week 6: Dance (Traditional South African dance styles)
- Week 7: Choreographing a group sequence
- Week 8: Assessment

## Term 3: Visual Arts & Music
- Week 1: Visual Arts (3D Construction: Found objects)
- Week 2: Visual Arts (Printmaking: Lino/Stenciling)
- Week 3: Visual Arts (Painting: Contemporary styles)
- Week 4: Music (Composition: Simple melodies)
- Week 5: Music (Musical instruments: Global context)
- Week 6: Music (Collaborative music making)
- Week 7: Integrating Art and Music
- Week 8: Assessment

## Term 4: Drama & Dance
- Week 1: Drama (Script writing: Conflict and climax)
- Week 2: Drama (Directing and staging basics)
- Week 3: Drama (Short play performance)
- Week 4: Dance (Contemporary and modern techniques)
- Week 5: Dance (Expression and performance skills)
- Week 6: Dance (Final production rehearsals)
- Week 7: Final Creative Arts Showcase
- Week 8: Final Assessment
