# Grade 7 English Home Language (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: Listening & Speaking (Group discussion: Literary themes)
- Week 2: Reading (Novel), Writing (Literary essay intro)
- Week 3: Language Structures (Verbs: Transitive and Intransitive)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (Prepared speech: Social issues)
- Week 5: Reading (Poetry: Figurative language), Writing (Sonnet/Lyric poem)
- Week 6: Language Structures (Phrases and Clauses: Relative clauses)
- Week 7: Reading (Folklore/Myths), Writing (Modern adaptation of a myth)
- Week 8: Language Structures (Punctuation: Colons and Semicolons)
- Week 9: Revision
- Week 10: Assessment

## Term 2
- Week 1: Listening & Speaking (Interviewing and reporting)
- Week 2: Reading (Newspaper/Magazine), Writing (Investigative report)
- Week 3: Language Structures (Modality: Modal verbs)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (Debate: Formal structure)
- Week 5: Reading (Drama: Stage directions/Dialogue), Writing (One-act play scene)
- Week 6: Language Structures (Tenses: Revision of all perfect tenses)
- Week 7: Reading (Informational text), Writing (Summary/Precis)
- Week 8: Language Structures (Active and Passive voice)
- Week 9: Revision
- Week 10: Assessment

## Term 3
- Week 1: Listening & Speaking (Oral presentation with visual aids)
- Week 2: Reading (Short stories), Writing (Narrative essay)
- Week 3: Language Structures (Direct and Indirect speech)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (Book review/Panel discussion)
- Week 5: Reading (Visual literacy: Cartoons/Ads), Writing (Ad campaign)
- Week 6: Language Structures (Conjunctions: Compound and Complex sentences)
- Week 7: Reading (Non-fiction: Biographies), Writing (Autobiographical sketch)
- Week 8: Language Structures (Vocabulary: Etymology/Prefixes/Suffixes)
- Week 9: Revision
- Week 10: Assessment

## Term 4
- Week 1: Listening & Speaking (Persuasive oral)
- Week 2: Reading (Literature revision: Novel and Drama)
- Week 3: Language Structures (Revision of all grammar and conventions)
- Week 4: Listening & Speaking (Role play: Conflict resolution/Mediation)
- Week 5: Reading (Public documents), Writing (Formal letter/Application)
- Week 6: Language Structures (Figurative language and Rhetoric)
- Week 7: Final Revision (Exam preparation)
- Week 8: Final Examination
- Week 9: Feedback and Moderation
- Week 10: Year-end activities
