# Grade 7 Life Orientation (CAPS)

## Term 1: Development of the self in society
- Week 1: Self-image and self-motivation
- Week 2: Interests, abilities, and potential
- Week 3: Peer pressure and group work
- Week 4: Bullying: Prevention and response
- Week 5: Physical Education (Basic fitness/Games)
- Week 6: Creative Arts (Drama: Mime and storytelling)
- Week 7: Creative Arts (Visual Arts: Painting)
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 2: Health, Social and Environmental Responsibility
- Week 1: Nutrition and a balanced diet
- Week 2: Communicable diseases (HIV/AIDS)
- Week 3: Environmental health (Cleanliness and waste)
- Week 4: Substance abuse education
- Week 5: Physical Education (Rhythmic movement)
- Week 6: Creative Arts (Music: African musical instruments)
- Week 7: Creative Arts (Visual Arts: Drawing)
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 3: Constitutional Rights and Responsibilities
- Week 1: Human rights and the Bill of Rights
- Week 2: Cultural diversity and religious festivals
- Week 3: National symbols and ceremonies
- Week 4: Democratic participation
- Week 5: Physical Education (Athletics/Summer sports)
- Week 6: Creative Arts (Drama: Role play)
- Week 7: Creative Arts (Visual Arts: Sculpture)
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 4: World of Work and Safety
- Week 1: Careers and subject choices
- Week 2: Time management and study skills
- Week 3: Traffic safety and passenger behavior
- Week 4: Physical Education (Swimming/Games)
- Week 5: Creative Arts (Music: Folk and modern songs)
- Week 6: Creative Arts (Visual Arts: Design/Media)
- Week 7: Final Revision
- Week 8: Final Assessment
