# Grade 7 Natural Sciences (CAPS)

## Term 1: Life and Living
- Week 1: The Biosphere (Concept and structure)
- Week 2: Biodiversity (Classification of living things)
- Week 3: Sexual reproduction in plants
- Week 4: Human reproduction (Overview)
- Week 5: Variation and adaptation
- Week 6: Human systems (Digestive/Respiratory)
- Week 7: Microorganisms (Health impact)
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 2: Matter and Materials
- Week 1: Properties of materials (Solids, liquids, gases)
- Week 2: Separation of mixtures (Physical methods)
- Week 3: Acids, bases, and neutrals
- Week 4: Introduction to the Periodic Table
- Week 5: Metals and non-metals
- Week 6: Processing of materials
- Week 7: Sustainability in material use
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 3: Energy and Change
- Week 1: Sources of energy (Renewable/Non-renewable)
- Week 2: Potential and Kinetic energy
- Week 3: Heat transfer (Conduction, Convection, Radiation)
- Week 4: Insulation and energy efficiency
- Week 5: Energy and the national grid
- Week 6: Electricity (Circuits and safety)
- Week 7: Visible light and color
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 4: Earth and Beyond
- Week 1: Relationship of the Sun to Earth
- Week 2: Relationship of the Moon to Earth
- Week 3: Tides and their causes
- Week 4: Historical development of astronomy
- Week 5: Modern space exploration
- Week 6: Earth's atmosphere
- Week 7: Global warming and climate change
- Week 8: Final Assessment
