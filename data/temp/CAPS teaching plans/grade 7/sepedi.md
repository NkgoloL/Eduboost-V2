# Grade 7 Sepedi First Additional Language (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: Madume le polelo ya leago (Social language and formal greetings)
- Week 2: Go bala dikanegelo (Reading short stories)
- Week 3: Go ngwala ditemana tša go hlalosa (Descriptive writing)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Grammar: Maina le Maatla)
- Week 5: Bophelo le maphelo (Health and wellness)
- Week 6: Polelo ya baswa (Youth language and culture)
- Week 7: Go theeletša direto (Traditional praises)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko (Assessment)

## Term 2
- Week 1: Tikologo le tšhomišo ya matlotlo (Environment and resources)
- Week 2: Go bala dikanegelo tša go ruta (Fables and lessons)
- Week 3: Go ngwala mangwalo a semmušo (Formal letters)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Grammar: Mašito le Mašala)
- Week 5: Dipalangwa le kgokagano (Transport and communication)
- Week 6: Boemo bja boso (Weather and climate change)
- Week 7: Go bolela (Debating current issues)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko (Assessment)

## Term 3
- Week 1: Dijo le meetlo (Food and traditions)
- Week 2: Go bala ditaba tša naga (National news)
- Week 3: Go ngwala direto (Writing poetry)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Grammar: Mahlatse le Makgao)
- Week 5: Dipapadi le boitšhidullo (Sports and exercise)
- Week 6: Melao le ditshwanelo (Laws and rights)
- Week 7: Go theeletša dikanegelo tša setšo (Traditional stories)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko (Assessment)

## Term 4
- Week 1: Setšo le boitšhupo (Culture and identity)
- Week 2: Go bala dipoelo tša tsebo (Informational texts)
- Week 3: Go ngwala (Creative writing/Essays)
- Week 4: Sebopego sa polelo (Revision of all grammar)
- Week 5: Bokamoso le ditiro (Future and careers)
- Week 6: Go bolela ka molomo (Final presentations)
- Week 7: Boitokišetšo bja ditlhahlobo (Exam prep)
- Week 8: Tshekatsheko ya mafelelo (Final assessment)
