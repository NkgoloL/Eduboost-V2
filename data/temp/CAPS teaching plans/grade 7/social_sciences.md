# Grade 7 Social Sciences (CAPS)

## Term 1: Geography - Map skills
- Week 1: Local maps and street maps
- Week 2: Sketch maps and landmarks
- Week 3: Latitude and longitude (Degrees and minutes)
- Week 4: Map scales and distance calculations
- Week 5: Relief and contours
- Week 6: Orthophoto maps
- Week 7: Interpreting diverse map types
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 2: History - The Trans-Atlantic Slave Trade
- Week 1: Introduction to slavery
- Week 2: West Africa before the slave trade
- Week 3: The nature of the slave trade (Middle Passage)
- Week 4: Impact of slavery on West Africa
- Week 5: Impact of slavery on the Americas
- Week 6: Resistance to slavery
- Week 7: Abolition of the slave trade
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 3: Geography - Earthquakes and Volcanoes
- Week 1: Structure of the Earth
- Week 2: Plate tectonics
- Week 3: Causes and effects of earthquakes
- Week 4: Measuring earthquakes (Richter scale)
- Week 5: Causes and types of volcanoes
- Week 6: Living with volcanoes and earthquakes
- Week 7: Case studies of natural disasters
- Week 8: Revision & Assessment

## Term 4: History - Colonization of the Cape
- Week 1: Indigenous people of the Cape (Khoisan)
- Week 2: The Dutch East India Company (VOC) at the Cape
- Week 3: Impact of colonization on indigenous people
- Week 4: Slave labor at the Cape
- Week 5: Frontiers and conflict in the Cape Colony
- Week 6: Significance of Cape history today
- Week 7: Final Revision
- Week 8: Final Assessment
