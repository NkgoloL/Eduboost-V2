# Grade R English Home Language (CAPS)

## Term 1: Listening and Speaking
- Week 1: Classroom routines and greetings
- Week 2: Listening to short stories
- Week 3: Following simple instructions
- Week 4: Emergent Reading (Handling a book)
- Week 5: Phonological Awareness (Rhyming words)
- Week 6: Visual Literacy (Looking at pictures)
- Week 7: Vocabulary building (My Body)
- Week 8: Assessment (Oral)

## Term 2: Emergent Literacy
- Week 1: Identifying initial sounds
- Week 2: Storytelling (Retelling a story)
- Week 3: Emergent Writing (Scribbling and drawing)
- Week 4: Letter-sound recognition (a, s, t)
- Week 5: Fine motor skills (Tracing)
- Week 6: Vocabulary (My Home)
- Week 7: Group discussion (My Family)
- Week 8: Assessment (Oral and fine motor)

## Term 3: Reading and Writing
- Week 1: Recognising own name
- Week 2: Matching letters and sounds
- Week 3: Reading environmental print (Signs)
- Week 4: Creative writing (Drawing a story)
- Week 5: Vocabulary (Animals)
- Week 6: Segmenting words into syllables
- Week 7: Poetry and Action songs
- Week 8: Assessment

## Term 4: Consolidation
- Week 1: Advanced phonological awareness
- Week 2: "Reading" familiar books
- Week 3: Writing simple letters and patterns
- Week 4: Role play and drama
- Week 5: Vocabulary (Transport)
- Week 6: Preparing for Grade 1 (Transition)
- Week 7: Year-end literacy fun
- Week 8: Final Assessment
