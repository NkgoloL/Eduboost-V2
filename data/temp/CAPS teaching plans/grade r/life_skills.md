# Grade R Life Skills (CAPS)

## Term 1: Personal and Social Well-being
- Week 1: Me and my classroom
- Week 2: Personal hygiene (Washing hands)
- Week 3: Emotions (Happy, Sad, Angry)
- Week 4: Manners and sharing
- Week 5: Physical Education (Big muscle play)
- Week 6: Creative Arts (Painting and messy play)
- Week 7: Safety in the classroom
- Week 8: Assessment

## Term 2: Health and Environment
- Week 1: Healthy eating (Fruit and veg)
- Week 2: My senses (Taste, Smell, Touch)
- Week 3: Safety at home
- Week 4: Water play and conservation
- Week 5: Physical Education (Balance and coordination)
- Week 6: Creative Arts (Playdough and construction)
- Week 7: The environment (Plants and trees)
- Week 8: Assessment

## Term 3: Community and Rights
- Week 1: People who help us (Doctors, Police)
- Week 2: Religious and cultural festivals
- Week 3: National symbols (The flag)
- Week 4: My rights and responsibilities
- Week 5: Physical Education (Ball skills)
- Week 6: Creative Arts (Singing and dancing)
- Week 7: Animals and their babies
- Week 8: Assessment

## Term 4: Careers and Safety
- Week 1: Jobs people do
- Week 2: Road safety (Traffic lights)
- Week 3: Danger and fire safety
- Week 4: Physical Education (Outdoor games)
- Week 5: Creative Arts (Puppetry and drama)
- Week 6: Celebration and transition to Grade 1
- Week 7: Year-end fun
- Week 8: Final Assessment
