# Grade R Mathematics (CAPS)

## Term 1: Numbers and Counting
- Week 1: Introduction to numbers 1-5 (Counting objects)
- Week 2: Recognising number symbols 1-3
- Week 3: Comparing collections (More/Less)
- Week 4: Sorting by color and size
- Week 5: Basic shapes (Circles and Squares)
- Week 6: Position (On top of, under)
- Week 7: Patterns (AB patterns with colors)
- Week 8: Revision and play-based assessment

## Term 2: Operations and Patterns
- Week 1: Numbers 1-10 (Counting forwards)
- Week 2: Number symbols 4-6
- Week 3: Simple addition (Adding one more)
- Week 4: Shapes (Triangles and Rectangles)
- Week 5: Measurement (Long and Short)
- Week 6: Time (Morning, Afternoon, Night)
- Week 7: Complex patterns (AABB)
- Week 8: Revision and play-based assessment

## Term 3: Space and Shape
- Week 1: Number symbols 7-10
- Week 2: Counting backwards from 5
- Week 3: Sharing (Equal groups)
- Week 4: 3D objects (Balls and Boxes)
- Week 5: Measurement (Heavy and Light)
- Week 6: Data handling (Sorting by kind)
- Week 7: Directions (Left and Right)
- Week 8: Revision and play-based assessment

## Term 4: Consolidation
- Week 1: Numbers 1-10 (Fluency)
- Week 2: Recognising symbols in the environment
- Week 3: Problem solving (Simple story sums)
- Week 4: Shapes and Patterns (Revision)
- Week 5: Capacity (Full and Empty)
- Week 6: Money (Recognising coins)
- Week 7: Year-end mathematics fun
- Week 8: Final Assessment
