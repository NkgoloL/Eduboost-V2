# Grade R Sepedi First Additional Language (CAPS)

## Term 1
- Week 1: Madume le boitsebišo (Greetings)
- Week 2: Ke nna mang? (Who am I?)
- Week 3: Go theeletša dikanegelo (Listening to stories)
- Week 4: Dikoša tša bana (Children's songs)
- Week 5: Mmele wa ka (My body)
- Week 6: Mebala (Colors)
- Week 7: Go hlaola dilo (Sorting objects)
- Week 8: Assessment

## Term 2
- Week 1: Lapa la ka (My family)
- Week 2: Dijo (Food)
- Week 3: Go bala dikanegelo ka seswantšho (Picture stories)
- Week 4: Diphoofolo tša gae (Domestic animals)
- Week 5: Diaparo (Clothes)
- Week 6: Go ngwala (Emergent writing)
- Week 7: Metshameko (Games)
- Week 8: Assessment

## Term 3
- Week 1: Sekolo sa ka (My school)
- Week 2: Dinomoro (Numbers 1-5)
- Week 3: Diphoofolo tša naga (Wild animals)
- Week 4: Boemo bja boso (Weather)
- Week 5: Dipalangwa (Transport)
- Week 6: Maphelo (Health)
- Week 7: Direto (Poetry)
- Week 8: Assessment

## Term 4
- Week 1: Go ithuta (Learning)
- Week 2: Mekete (Celebrations)
- Week 3: Ditlhaba (Nature)
- Week 4: Poledišano (Conversation)
- Week 5: Go itokišetša Grade 1 (Transition)
- Week 6: Bokamoso (Future)
- Week 7: Boitokišetšo bja mafelelo
- Week 8: Final Assessment
